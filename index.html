The Revolution of League of Legends

October 11, 2024 by Kevin Wang

League of Legends (LOL) is up tp now one of the most well-know video games in the esports world, with its complicated strategic gameplay. It started with Riot Games in 2009 and it has constantly changed through regular updates, the introduction of new champions, and balancing changes that keep the gameplay fresh and competitve. League of Legend is a game that's remarkable in its versatility, as a game for normal player and a game for professional esports player alike. With more than 160 champions, each with their own skills and tactics, players are encouraged to try different roles and strategies, whether it's in solo queue or high-rank tournaments.
